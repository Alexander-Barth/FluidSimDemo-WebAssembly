Gray Scott equations implemented in Julia and compiled to WASM.

Equations

∂u ∂t  = Dᵤ ∇²u - u v² + f (1 - u)

∂v ∂t = Dᵥ ∇²v + u v² - (f + k) v

The domain is 256 x 256 grid point with a spatial resolution of Δx = 1 and a time step Δt = 1. At the boundary u and v are set to zero.
